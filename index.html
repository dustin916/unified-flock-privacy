Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Unified Flock ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web platform (collectively, the "Service"). By using Unified Flock, you agree to the terms of this policy.

If you have questions, contact us at dustin.tech22@gmail.com.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account we collect your first name, last name, username, email address, and a securely hashed password. in plain text.

Profile Information (option

You may choose to provide a profile photo, bio, phone number, location, and date of birth. All of these fields are voluntary.

Church Membership Data

We store your church affiliations, your role within each church (member, leader, or admin), and the date you joined.

User-Generated Content

We store content you create within the Service, including prayer requests, announcements, chat messages, and join requests.

Prayer requests may be submitted anonymously at your option. When submitted anonymously, your name is hidden from other members. However, the request remains linked to your account so that you may edit or delete it at any time.

Payment Information

If you subscribe to a paid plan, payment processing is handled by Stripe. We do not store your credit card number or full payment details. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy. We may retain your billing email and subscription status.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and church memberships
  • To deliver the core features of the Service (chat, prayer requests, events, announcements)
  • To send you in-app notifications and, when enabled, email notifications for password resets
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions through Stripe
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and moderate content
  • To improve and maintain the security and performance of the Service

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

Within Your Church

Information you choose to make visible (name, email, phone) is shared with other members of your church according to your privacy settings. Church admins can always see full member information regardless of your privacy settings.

Email Service

We use Gmail's SMTP service to send password reset emails. Your email address is transmitted to deliver this message.

Payment Processor

Billing information is processed by Stripe. We share only what is necessary to complete your transaction.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal process.

4. Your Privacy Controls

You control how much of your information is visible to other members:

  • Show/hide your full name — members see your username instead if hidden
  • Show/hide your email address — hidden from other members by default
  • Show/hide your phone number — hidden from other members by default
  • Anonymous prayer requests — submit prayer requests without your name attached

These settings can be changed at any time in your Profile settings.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time from the app's Settings screen or Profile page. When you delete your account:

  • Your profile and personal information are permanently removed
  • Your church memberships are removed
  • Your prayer requests are removed
  • Your chat messages are removed
  • Any churches you own are deleted along with all their data

6. Security

  • Passwords are hashed using Django's PBKDF2 algorithm — never stored in plain text
  • All production traffic is encrypted via HTTPS
  • API access requires authentication tokens
  • Database credentials and secret keys are stored in environment variables, not in code

7. Children's Privacy

Unified Flock is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Users are required to confirm they are 13 years of age or older during account registration. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information without verifiable parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us at dustin.tech22@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
